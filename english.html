ABOUT US

In 2010, it started as a Cardiology clinic and due to the relationship between diet and cardiovascular disease, the nutrition area was linked, thus, the Non-Invasive Cardiology Center of Antigua Guatemala was born. With this alliance, comprehensive control was achieved.

Likewise, the incorporation of a cardiovascular product pharmacy in order to provide the patient with a better service and ensure the quality of the drugs they consume.

Despite the fact that one of our founders is a clinical cardiologist and cardiovascular intensivist for the global management of the patient, the need to create the area of ​​Internal Medicine was generated.

For many years we realized the need for a clinical laboratory with high-level hygiene and biosafety standards in the Sacatepequez area.

And it was not until 2021 when it was decided to open the Clinical Laboratory, this in order to be able to assure the patient controlled, accurate quality results and in the shortest possible time.

With this laboratory we close the circle to ensure a high quality service to our patients.

What we want?

Antigua cardiology center is a company with 11 years of experience in the health area, where its main objective is the search for excellence in the diagnosis and treatment of the patient.

From the beginning we have been aware that the economic situation in our country is not equitable, so we support the most needy and various public and private institutions (NGOs), this in the area of consultations, specific examinations and now also in the clinical laboratory .
